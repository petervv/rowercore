RowerCore — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

RowerCore ("the app", "we", "us") is a training companion for Bluetooth FTMS-compatible rowing machines. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile data — name, gender, date of birth, height, and weight that you enter voluntarily in the app.
  • Workout data — session metrics (distance, pace, strokes per minute, heart rate, duration) recorded during training sessions.
  • Device identifiers — the Bluetooth name and hardware ID of your rowing machine and heart rate monitor, stored so the app can reconnect automatically.
  • Strava tokens — if you connect Strava, your OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored in the device's secure keystore.
  • Purchase status — whether you have purchased RowerCore Pro, stored locally on the device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Profile and workout data are used solely to display statistics and power your training sessions within the app.
  • Strava tokens are used exclusively to upload your completed workouts to your Strava account at your request.
  • No personal data is sold, rented, or shared with third-party advertisers.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Profile data and device preferences are stored in the app's private sandboxed storage. Strava tokens are stored in the platform's secure keystore (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain). We do not operate a user account system or store your personal data on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Strava — If you connect Strava, your workout data is sent to Strava in TCX format according to Strava's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play (in-app purchases) — Purchase transactions are processed by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Diagnostic Data

With your consent, anonymised Bluetooth device diagnostic reports may be sent to our server to help us improve compatibility. These reports contain device capability data only and no personally identifiable information.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 years of age and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at info@vewello.com.
